Discover Bihar

Bihar, the land of ancient civilizations, offers a rich cultural and historical heritage. Explore the beauty of Bihar and its many attractions.

Top Attractions

  • Mahabodhi Temple
  • Nalanda University
  • Vikramshila Ruins
  • Patna Museum
  • Rajgir Hot Springs

Culture

Bihar boasts a vibrant culture with festivals like Chhath Puja and traditional dance forms like Jat-Jatin and Kathak. The state is known for its classical music and folk arts.

Food

Experience the flavors of Bihar with dishes like Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, and Thekua. Don't miss the traditional sweets like Khaja and Tilkut.
