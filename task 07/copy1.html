Discover Bihar

Bihar, the land of ancient civilizations, offers a rich cultural and historical heritage. Explore the beauty of Bihar and its many attractions.

Bihar, located in eastern India, is a land of historical and cultural significance. Known for ancient cities like Nalanda and Bodh Gaya, Bihar boasts a rich heritage. The state is famous for its festivals like Chhath Puja and its unique cuisine, including dishes like Litti Chokha and Sattu Paratha. Bihar offers a blend of historical landmarks, vibrant culture, and delightful culinary experiences, making it a captivating destination for visitors.


Top Attractions

  • Mahabodhi Temple
  • Nalanda University
  • Vikramshila Ruins
  • Patna Museum
  • Rajgir Hot Springs
  • Bihar Sharif
  • Takht Sri Patna Sahib

Culture

Bihar boasts a vibrant culture with festivals like Eid-ul-fitr,Eid-ui-adha, Chhath Puja,Holi,Diwali and traditional dance forms like Jat-Jatin and Kathak. The state is known for its classical music and folk arts.


Food

Experience the flavors of Bihar with dishes like Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, and Thekua. Don't miss the traditional sweets like Khaja and Tilkut.
